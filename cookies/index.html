INTERWEB GIRL

the best chocolate chip cookies known to man

 go back !



WARNING!! There's a lot of dough, do not bake everything at once!!!
(or you can, but you'll need a really big baking sheet)

what you need

1 cup of unsalted butter (UNSALTED BUTTER IS JUST BETTER!!)

1 cup of sugar

1 cup of packed brown sugar

2 tsp of vanilla extract

2 eggs

3 cups of all-purpose flour

1 tsp baking soda

1/2 tsp baking powder

1 tsp salt

2 cups of chocolate chips (just throw the whole bag in!!)


instructions!!


preheat the oven to 375F

put some parchment paper on the counter

in a medium bowl, mix your flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt

in another bowl, mix your butter and sugar until theyre combined

then beat in your eggs and vanilla until its all mixed together

put it all into one bowl and mix it together

add the chocolate chips once the dough is all together and mix them in

make all your dough into balls and put them on a baking sheet with parchment paper on it
(i reccomend making each of the balls about 3 tbsp, or [INSERT GRAMS HERE])

throw it in the oven for 8-10 minutes, take them out when they're just barely turning brown
(they will not look done! but they are!!)

take it out and let it sit for a few minutes before moving them to a cooling rack or plate

wait about 15 minutes or so and now you can eat it !

if you try eating them before that then they'll be really hard to eat and they'll be falling apart and stuff, but yeah!!

to store it, put it in a bag and into the fridge, try to get all the oxygen out of the bag
(i havent tested it but that's generally a good way to store perishable food, so do that)
